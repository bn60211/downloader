Doodly for Computer

Doodly is a powerful tool that allows you to design complete videos, specifically focusing on cartoon videos rather than regular ones. While it may not have a robust editor, it offers numerous features that make it exceptional, including a variety of ready-to-use shapes. These shapes can be incorporated into your videos, and the program boasts a simple interface that's easy to navigate.

The interface is divided into several sections, each crucial in its own right. In the center, you'll find the workspace where you can design your video. Initially, you'll need to understand the timeline, located at the bottom of the program. This timeline is the video’s path, where you design your boards or shapes and determine when each will appear and disappear. This allows you to arrange your elements as you wish, creating a seamless video. Select the desired time, and a blank board will appear. On the left side, you'll find a list of your shapes, where you can choose and place them onto the workspace.

Next, move to the subsequent time slots and add the shapes you want, following this sequence to build your video. If you wish to modify any properties, whether of a specific shape or the project itself, head to the right side of the program, where you'll find a list of properties for any part you're working on. At the top, there's a typical menu bar found in most programs, like "File," which you can use to save your project or open an existing one.
